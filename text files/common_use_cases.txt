==================================================
SALES AND CRM
==================================================

USE CASE: Lead Capture and Distribution
----------------------------------------------
Manual process:
- Leads come in via website form, email, phone
- Someone manually enters into CRM
- Sales manager manually assigns to reps
- Rep manually notified
- Follow-up reminders set manually

Time: 10-15 minutes per lead × 50 leads/week = 8-12 hours/week

Automation solution:
- Website form → auto-create CRM lead
- Auto-enrich with company data (Clearbit, etc.)
- Auto-score based on fit criteria
- Auto-assign to appropriate rep (round-robin or territory-based)
- Auto-notify rep in Slack with lead details
- Auto-schedule follow-up task in CRM

Time after automation: 0-1 hours/week (spot checks only)
Savings: 7-11 hours/week
Complexity: Simple-Medium
Tools: Website forms, CRM (HubSpot/Salesforce), Slack, Clearbit

==================================================
IDENTIFYING YOUR AUTOMATION OPPORTUNITIES
==================================================

QUESTIONS TO ASK
----------------------------------------------
1. What tasks do you or your team do repeatedly (daily, weekly, monthly)?
2. What takes a surprisingly long time for how simple it seems?
3. What falls through the cracks due to manual tracking?
4. What requires copying data from one system to another?
5. What do you wish happened automatically?
6. What errors occur due to manual data entry?
7. What reports take hours to create?
8. What notifications or reminders are sent manually?
9. What approvals require email chains?
10. What coordination requires back-and-forth communication?

HIGH-ROI INDICATORS
----------------------------------------------
✓ High frequency (done often)
✓ Time-consuming (takes significant time)
✓ Rule-based (clear decision criteria)
✓ Multi-system (requires accessing multiple tools)
✓ Error-prone (manual entry or calculation)
✓ Creates bottlenecks (delays other work)
✓ Scales poorly (more work as business grows)

AUTOMATION SELECTION FRAMEWORK
----------------------------------------------
Score potential automations on:

1. Time savings potential (1-5)
2. Frequency (1-5)
3. Impact on business (1-5)
4. Implementation complexity (1-5, lower is better)
5. Number of people affected (1-5)

Prioritize high total scores.
