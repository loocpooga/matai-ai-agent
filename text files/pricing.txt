Pricing Philosophy
- Pricing is based on complexity, scope, and ongoing support.
- Most projects include discovery, build, testing, deployment, and documentation.
- Monthly plans cover hosting, monitoring, fixes, and small improvements.

Packages

Starter
- Best for: One simple automation or workflow
- Setup fee: $750
- Monthly: $150 per month
- Includes:
  - One automation or workflow
  - Basic error handling and logging
  - One hour of support per month

Growth
- Best for: Multiple automations and basic reporting
- Setup fee: $2,500
- Monthly: $500 per month
- Includes:
  - Up to four automations or workflows
  - One basic dashboard or reporting pipeline
  - Monitoring and alerting
  - Three hours of support per month

Scale
- Best for: Ongoing automation and reporting needs
- Setup fee: $5,000 or more
- Monthly: $1,250 or more per month
- Includes:
  - Six or more automations or workflows
  - Multi-source reporting pipelines
  - Higher reliability monitoring
  - Six hours of support per month
